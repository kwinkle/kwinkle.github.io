About Me

I'm currently finishing up my PhD on socially assistive robotics as part of the ECHOS group at the Bristol Robotics Laboratory, where I'm also a Research Assisant on the RoboTIPS project.

Latest Updates

June 2020 ~ New publication

Our paper "In-Situ Learning from a Domain Expert for Real World Socially Assistive Robot Deployment" was accepted for this year's RSS conference. The paper details the technical architecture of my couch to 5km robot fitness coach and presents some preliminary evaluation results. My 5 minute video talk will be uploaded and available after the virutal conference event.

Jan 2020 ~ Invited Talk

I travelled to Colorado, USA to give a talk on my work and its ethical implications ("Designing Persuasive Robots, Ethically") at the invitation of Dr Tom Williams; supported by Mines' Computer Science department and the Daniels Fund.

Nov 2019 ~ Workshop

I was invited to take part in the Future Digileaders career workshop at KTH, so I got to spend a couple of days in Stockholm, Sweden.
